### Problem 3. The biggest of Three
*	Write a script that finds the biggest of three numbers.
*	Use nested `if` statements.

_Examples:_

| a    | b    | c    | biggest |
|------|------|------|:-------:|
| 5    | 2    | 2    | 5       |
| -2   | -2   | 1    | 1       |
| -2   | 4    | 3    | 4       |
| 0    | -2.5 | 5    | 5       |
| -0.1 | -0.5 | -1.1 | -0.1    |
