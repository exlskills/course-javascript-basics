### Problem 7. The biggest of five numbers
*	Write a script that finds the greatest of given 5 variables.
*	Use nested `if` statements.

_Examples:_

| a    | b    | c    |  d |   e  | biggest |
|------|------|------|:--:|:----:|---------|
| 5    | 2    | 2    | 4  | 1    | 5       |
| -2   | -22  | 1    | 0  | 0    | 1       |
| -2   | 4    | 3    | 2  | 0    | 4       |
| 0    | -2.5 | 0    | 5  | 5    | 5       |
| -3   | -0.5 | -1.1 | -2 | -0.1 | -0.1    |
